Season 1

Season 1 introduces us to the main characters: Will, Mike, Dustin, and Lucas. Will goes missing, and his mother, Joyce, begins receiving strange messages through a series of colored lights she arranges in her house. Eleven, a young girl with telekinetic abilities, joins the three remaining boys on their search for Will. Ultimately, they discover an alternate universe called the Upside Down, where Eleven and the boys find Will and battle the Demogorgon.

Season 2

Season 2 picks up roughly a year after the events of Season 1. Eleven, who went missing after the battle with the Demogorgon in Season 1, is secretly living with police cheif Jim Hopper. She is in hiding because of her powers, and her friends don't know if she is even alive. Unfortunately, Will is still a target for the denizens of the Upside Down, and a new enemy, The Mind Flayer, is after him. Ultimately, Eleven is reunited with the boys, and together they take on the Mind Flayer, banishing it back to the Upside Down.

Season 3

The most recent season finds the Russians in Hawkins, Indiana, where they have built a secret lab under the new Starcourt Mall. From here, they are reopening the portal to the Upside Down. The Mind Flayer is released, and begins taking over the bodies of Hawkins' residents. Apparently, this town just can't catch a break. Supernatural hijinks ensue, and ultimately the kids win once more, but Police Cheif Hopper may have been killed in the final battle. We'll have to wait for Season 4...
