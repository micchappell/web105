The Challenge

We want our featured guests to look great on camera, but not everyone knows how to use lighting and camera position to look their best.

The Solution

Lighting is one of the biggest problems when you don't have the proper equipment. Interior, overhead lights can create hotspots on the forehead, bridge of the nose, and cheeks. And even worse, overhead lighting can create dark shadows that hide the subjects eyes.
