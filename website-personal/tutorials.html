Tutorials

While I haven't had time to do any tutorials recently, here's a selection of some techniques that are still relevant and applicable in 2019.

Create an Animated Logo in After Effects

In this tutorial, we'll do some image manipulation in Photoshop to create a logo. Then we'll import the logo to After Effects, where we'll add some styling to create a 3D look. We'll create a 3D environment for our logo by cutting up a 2D photo and positioning it in 3D space, and then we'll add a few finishing touches to blend all of the elements together.

 

Make an Iceberg in Photoshop and After Effects

Learn how to take a collection of different iceberg photos and blend them in Photoshop to create one complete iceberg. Then we'll composite the iceberg in an After Effects compostion with a sky and a simulated water surface.

 

Procedural Luma Matte Keying in After Effects

How do you do a green screen key without a green screen? Use a white wall and this cool trick in After Effects.
